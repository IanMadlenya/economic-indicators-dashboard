About

This dashboard provide city leaders, businesses and residents with a quick snapshot of the San Jose economy.

The indicators will be updated on a quarterly basis. Email us to subscribe to announcements about data releases and new features.

By making economic data easily accessible format, we aim to equip the community with the best possible information to understand the economy and engage in policy discussions.

Give Us Your Feedback

How This Dashboard Was Made

This dashboard was a unique collaboration between Office of Economic Development staff and dedicated volunteers from Code for San Jose. The core team consisted of:

  • Laurie Reynolds - Lead Developer (Code for San Jose volunteer)
  • Danny Shapira - Front-End Developer (Code for San Jose volunteer)
  • Michelle Thong - Project Manager, Office of Economic Development

The indicator data are compiled by the Office of Economic Development using data from public and private sources. Data sources for each indicator are revealed by positioning a cursor over the "information" symbol, "i".

Technologies used:

  • D3 - Data Driven Documents
  • NVD3 - Re-usable charts for d3.js
  • Hosted using Github pages
  • Twitter Bootstrap

All the data used for this site can be downloaded on the City of San Jose's Open Data PortalV.
